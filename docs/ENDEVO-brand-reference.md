## Identity

- Company: **ENDevo** (styled "ENDevo")
- Slogan: **PLAN. PROTECT. PEACE.**
- Tagline: **Live Fully. Die Ready.** (site uses the period form; our B2C uses the
  founder-locked tilde "Live Fully ~ Die Ready." per BRAND-VOICE-RULES.md)
- Positioning: **"Simplifying Legacy Readiness & Digital Resilience"**
- One-liner: "Empowering people to prepare with clarity, protect digital
  footprints, and create peace of mind for generations."
- Founder: **Niki Weiss**, title **Digital Legacy Thanatologist**, Founder / CEO.
- Mission (verbatim): "normalize conversations about death, dying and end-of-life
  planning, break down complex decision-making into actionable steps and provide
  cutting-edge tools to prepare for the inevitable, so we can live fully and die
  ready."

## The Framework (THE canonical model — align the app to this)

**Your Beliefs at the center**, then four key areas around it. From endevo.life
"Our Comprehensive Framework": *"Legacy planning isn't one-dimensional. We center
your beliefs, then build around them with four key areas that make your plan
complete."*

| Domain | Official one-line description |
|---|---|
| **Your Beliefs** (center) | The foundation of every decision: your values, wishes, and priorities. |
| **Legal** | Protect your rights and ensure your documents reflect your intentions. |
| **Financial** | Secure your assets and provide clarity for future generations. |
| **Physical** | Address care, health, and personal needs with confidence. |
| **Digital** | Organize and safeguard your online presence and digital assets. |

> NOTE for the app: our content library currently has legal/financial/physical/

## Core Values — C.L.A.R.I.T.Y.

- **C**ompassion: Honor humanity while simplifying the complex. Empathy and
  understanding in every situation.
- **L**egacy Readiness: Act today to safeguard families, organizations, and
  communities for tomorrow's challenges.
- **A**ction & Empowerment: Educate, project manage, and empower people to act
  with confidence and clarity.
- **R**esilience (Digital & Human): Protect identities, assets, and digital
  footprints with comprehensive safeguards.
- **I**nnovation with Integrity: Harness AI and emerging tech responsibly to
  serve human needs and values.
- **T**eamwork & Collaboration: Build systemic resilience with trusted partners
  and connected communities.
- **Y**esterday, Today, Tomorrow: Honor the past, strengthen the present, secure
  the future for generations.

## The 4-step "Path to Peace of Mind" (their onboarding framing)

1. Begin with the **Peace of Mind Assessment**, a quick check-up that highlights
   gaps and gives you a clear readiness score.
2. Use the AI-driven platform to gather your most important information in one place.
3. Gain access to guided support, including one-on-one sessions.
4. Securely share your plan with trusted family members or designated contacts.

> Our B2C app mirrors this: assessment → plan → (paid) guide → share/download.
> Consider adopting the term **"Peace of Mind Assessment"** and a **readiness
> score**, and **"Path to Peace of Mind"** framing, to align with the parent brand.
