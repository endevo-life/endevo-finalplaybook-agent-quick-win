Growth/Marketing Head's strategy for MyFinalPlaybook (2026-07-06). Drives landing
page + upgrade copy + video content.

**Core positioning insight:** competitors (Hospice Nurse Penny 4.2M, Hospice Nurse
Julie 3.5M, Alex Ferrari 300M downloads, Kim Vopni $215K/mo) all cover PHYSICAL
death. **Nobody owns DIGITAL death** — that's our lane. A TEDx on the digital
angle hit 3M+ views in <9 months; digital framing converts FASTER.

**The product is FEAR REDUCTION.** Growth came from people saying "I'm no longer
worried about death after watching." Demand is latent, not manufactured — the
audience is already searching. Landing/upgrade copy should reduce anxiety, not
sell features.

**Persona-led:** Niki publishes as "The Digital Thanatologist" (credentialed
title = trust signal, like Hospice Nurse / Vagina Coach), NOT as ENDevo. Note:
the APP is de-branded/neutral (MyFinalPlaybook), but MARKETING is Niki-persona-led.
Keep that distinction — app content stays expert-agnostic; marketing is Niki.

**Content model:** one anxiety = one short video (3-5/wk, phone-shot, direct to
camera). Each answers ONE high-fear question: "what happens to your Instagram
when you die," "who can unlock your phone," "can your family reach your crypto,"
"what happens to your work accounts." Every video ends pointing to the
**C.L.A.R.I.T.Y. framework** + the app as the single call-to-action.

**Slogan:** "Live Fully, Die Ready." Podcast: "Live Fully ~ Die Ready" (Season 2).

**Implication for the app:** the 10 high-anxiety video questions map directly to
our digital-legacy assessment questions (phone unlock, crypto, social, work
accounts) — content engine and marketing funnel should share the same question
set. The app IS the funnel destination.

**How to apply:** landing page + upgrade page should lead with fear-reduction
emotional hooks (not feature lists), reserve a video placeholder for Niki's
short-form, and make the app the clear CTA. C.L.A.R.I.T.Y. framework is the
branded method to align with. See [[business-goal-moat]] and [[deterministic-domain-model]].
